Introducing Phösa-29


Phösa-29 is a cyborg robot that carries heavy piles of items around and incorporates a variety of features and utilities. Scroll down to find out more!

Always Have Portable Storage

Phösa-29 is able to hold up to 5kg of items and is able to move from point to point. It is also capable of placing items using its artificial vision to predict where items belong. (There is also an egg storage on its right shoulder)

Never Have Your Items Melt Away

On top of storage, Phösa-29 also has a portable fridge mounted into its body. The fridge can preserve food items and ice, plus spherical items for more efficient storage.

Control Temperature With Ease

Phösa-29 implements a control system that automatically adjusts the temperature of the fridge into more warmer or cooler ones, depending on its use.

Heart Attack? Help Will Arrive

With a wireless pulse sensor attached, Phösa-29 can detect whether your heart acts abnormally, and will immediately signal help with the best of its abilities.

Ask And Receive Through AI

Phösa-29 can detect voices and respond to them accordingly using latest speech models. It also has the functionality of executing tasks based on the user's request accurately and quickly.
